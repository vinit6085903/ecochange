Our Commitment to Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Data Category What We Collect Purpose
Account Data Name, email, password, profile information Account creation, authentication, communication
Usage Data IP address, browser type, pages visited, features used Service improvement, analytics, security
Content Data Files, reports, dashboards, comments you create Providing core services, data processing
Payment Data Billing address, payment method (processed by secure providers) Transaction processing, subscription management
Technical Data Device information, cookies, log data Functionality, security, personalization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Communication: To send you important updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security risks
  • Analytics: To understand how our services are used and to improve them
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations and enforce our terms
Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on: (1) Your consent; (2) The necessity to perform our contract with you; (3) Our legitimate interests; (4) Compliance with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Whom What We Share Purpose
Service Providers Necessary data for specific services (hosting, payment processing, analytics) To enable third-party services that help us operate
Business Transfers User information as part of merger, acquisition, or sale Business continuity during corporate transactions
Legal Requirements Information required by law, court order, or government request To comply with legal obligations and protect rights
With Your Consent Specific data you authorize us to share To enable integrations or services you request

4. International Data Transfers

We operate globally and may transfer your information to countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. When we do, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions for countries with appropriate data protection laws
  • Your explicit consent for specific transfers

By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection rules than your country.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Security Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Employee Training: Security awareness training for all staff

While we strive to protect your data, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

Data Type Retention Period Notes
Account Information While account is active + 30 days after deletion Extended for legal compliance if required
Usage Data 24 months Aggregated analytics may be kept indefinitely
Financial Records 7 years Required for tax and accounting purposes
Support Communications 3 years To improve service and handle follow-ups

You can request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16 (or higher age depending on jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from or about a child, please contact us immediately.
