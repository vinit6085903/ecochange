Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period. No credit card is required to start the trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDC.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.
Do you offer discounts for teams?
Yes, we offer volume discounts for teams of 10+ users. Contact our sales team at sales@prosuite.com for custom pricing and enterprise agreements.
