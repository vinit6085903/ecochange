Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link will expire in 24 hours for security reasons.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription plan?

Yes, you can change your subscription plan at any time. Go to Account Settings > Subscription to view available plans. When upgrading, the new plan takes effect immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

How do I export my data?

You can export your data in several formats (CSV, JSON, PDF) from the Reports section. Go to Reports > Export Data, select the data range and format, then click "Export". Large exports may take a few minutes to process.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are GDPR compliant and never sell your data to third parties. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.
